Welcome to Samsung Digital Health!

Samsung Digital Health SDK includes 2 different packages.
Using the Health Data package is mandatory.

Refer to the following description: 

1. Health Data package

  - It enables you to read or write health data into the health data store in S Health. 
  - The library, tool, and related documents are included in the "SamsungDigitalHealthSDK/HealthData" folder. 
  
2. S Health Service package

  - It provides the tracker feature which enables you to post your tracker tile on the main screen of S Health and handle related events.
    It does not support reading or writing of health data in to the health data store.
  - The library, tool, and related documents are included in the "SamsungDigitalHealthSDK/SHealthService" folder.
  

** To work properly with S Health please apply for partner apps at http://developer.samsung.com/health after publishing your application that is connected to S Health. **
